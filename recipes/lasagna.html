Paneer MO:MO:

What is MO:MO:?

MO:MO: are bite-size dumplings made with a spoonful of stuffing wrapped in dough. MO:Mo: are usually steamed though they are sometimes fried or steam-fried. Meat or vegetables fillings becommes succulent as it produces an intensively flavore broth sealed inside the wrappers.

Ingredients

  • Panneer
  • Butter
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Dry Onions
  • Ginger and Garlic (paste)
  • Coriander (chopped)
  • Cumin Powder
  • Salt
  • Garam Masala
  • MO:MO: Masala

Cooking Method:

  1. Firstly, grate the paneer,
  2. Chop and boil the cabbage and carrot,
  3. Chop, dry and spring onions and keep them seperately,
  4. Mix the Paneer, Cabbage, Onions and Coriander.
  5. Prepare dough of refined wheat flour and make small lumps,
  6. Put mixturein it and make dumplings in the shapes as you like,
  7. It can be Steamed or Fried and once it becomes ready serve with tomato pickle.
